About This Project

This platform represents a breakthrough in automated gel electrophoresis analysis, combining advanced OpenCV computer vision techniques with sophisticated pattern recognition algorithms to provide accurate, fast, and reliable DNA analysis.

Gel electrophoresis is a laboratory technique used to separate DNA, RNA, or protein molecules based on their size and electrical charge. Our platform automates the analysis process, providing instant, accurate results with detailed disease predictions and forensic matching capabilities.
